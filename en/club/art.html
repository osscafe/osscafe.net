Modern Art Club

Cultivating the skills and knowledge to understand modern art, that's the Modern Art Club

What do we do?

Our activities, broadly...

  • Try analyzing various works by contemporary artists. (Discover new facets of art by examining contemporary works from a philosophical point of view.)
  • See works of art in museums and galleries!
  • Try your hand at making art! (For example, collaborate on the Nagashi-somen Project)

The club meets every other week on Saturday at 10 am.
