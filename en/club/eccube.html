EC-CUBE Club (Tokyo Users Group)

Let's learn, collaborate, and hack together with EC-CUBE.
EC-CUBE is one of Japan's most popular e-commerce CMSs. This is the clubroom for EC-CUBE users.

What do we do in the Open Source Clubroom?

Every other week we have a meet up to study and learn about open source. Beginners are welcome to ask questions and veterans are encouraged to help out in any way they can.

  • Chat and share your experiences with open source.
  • If you're just beginning or feel lost you can ask for help.
  • Get feedback on your open source projects.

The Open Source Clubroom is very open, so use it for things like this or bring your own ideas!
